Why Bubl can help you travel your own Terra Incognita...

What is "Terra Incognita"?

In early human history large regions of the world laid largely unmapped and these lands were labelled "Terra Incognita", translated, "unknown land". These wild lands lured humankind to make the journey into them, somewhat blindly, motivated by the thrill of exploration, dreams of great discovery and the desire to see conquer what lay beyond. Since early recorded history rumors of what lived in these open lands was not beyond fantasical notions, and the same lands labelled "Terra Incognita", in urban legend may also have been labelled "Here be dragons". Today, we who created Bubl believe that these early explorers would likely have been comforted in having a little bit more information about the lands they were travelling into, their fears of dragons quenched (or not!), locations of safe places to stay the night, and some insights into other tribes that lay within the boundaries they were embarking into.

Our development team is made up entirely of ex-junior high students who transitioned from junior high into our own "Terra Incognita" - highschool lands of mostly uncharted territory. We have heard the rumors about dragons, and navigated them. Like the early explorers who steeled themselves into unknown lands we too have traveled the halls of our schools, mapping them in our minds by our own experiences. We have known what it is like to explore a new place, and not know where your friends are or where you might belong.

Bubls mission is to help you connect with like-minded friends and begin building relationships you can trust before you even begin your own journey into the halls of your highschool. To help you find your tribe and chart some landmarks for your map as you begin your own journey into your own "Terra Incognita". It's as easy as finding your school, searching for a Bubl of other students that share your interests and joining their Bubl!
